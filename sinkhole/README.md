## Wait a Minute!
It doesn’t take a psychology degree to know that humans love clicking links—sometimes on purpose, sometimes by accident. When that happens (the most common route is through phishing), Pi-hole steps in and goes, “Hmm… I don’t know about that.” It filters malicious DNS queries and helps keep my home environment safer.
